• 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  1. put 1/2 cups all-purpose flour in the dry bowl
  2. add 1 cup white sugar
  3. add 1 teaspoon baking powder
  4. add 1/4 teaspoon salt
  5. mix 1/2 cup unsalted butter softened
  6. mix 2 large eggs
  7. pour 1/2 cup whole milk
  8. add 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
